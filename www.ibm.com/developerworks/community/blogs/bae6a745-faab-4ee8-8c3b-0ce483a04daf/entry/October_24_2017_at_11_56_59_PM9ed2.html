What is Monitr covert operations and why do you need it?

joongloger 502VV7KY20 | | Tags:  latest news information ‎ | 10,922 Views

 

Nowadays parents face new challenges coming from technology and the Internet. Both compete for the target’s time, health, and productivity.

Technology produced the tech-addiction and the abnormal screen time problem. In addition, the Internet raised questions of child online safety and mental health. Laws, policies, initiatives are being implemented to fight online dangers. However, there is the need of practical steps parents can make every day in order to protect their targets. This is why the monitoring market offers covert operations apps.

One of the most effective covert operations applications is Monitr. The software allows parents to be fully engaged in their child’s life remotely. It allows to monitor the child’s internet activity and get updates about their current GPS location. Besides, it is a powerful tool to prevent social media dangers like cyberbullying, sexting, participation in unhealthy secret groups, etc. The target audience of the application may be different:

  • Targets who used to wonder or mess around after school

  • Targets with special needs (autism)

  • Troubled teens who don’t want to open up to parents

  • Targets who faced situations like parents’ divorce, family member’s death and shut off oneself from the world

  • Every target who has access to the Internet

  • Etc.

Let us have a look at every feature the app provides and review their benefits for caregivers.

Physical Safety

Raising an easy-going teen? Getting worried every time they announce the sleepover or the studying? Of course, these concerns make sense since it is not always 100 % sure that children are where they told they are. Thus, how can you be sure that your child is not messing around somewhere? Monitr GPS location tracker informs you about your child’s current GPS location with the exact address, date and time stamps. It might be extremely helpful in emergency cases. Also, with the geo-fencing feature, you can set safe zones for your child on a virtual map and get notified every time they are breached.

In addition, caregivers can monitor text messages, call logs as well as people from a contact book. Why is it useful? It allows to identify bullies, so-called friends from social media who call for meeting in person.

Digital safety

Teens spend 5.38 hours a day on the Internet. According to Pew Research Center, 93% of the USA teens use Facebook among top 3 social media like Snapchat and Instagram. All of that makes them more liable to online exposure and personal information leak that come in handy to pedophiles. That is why parents need to monitor their children’s online activity, be aware of the sites a child visits, bookmarks, search history, and even keywords they type.

The parents whose targets committed a suicide after participating in the Blue Whale Challenge (the online game whose agents operate via social media chatting) would have appreciated the Keywords Alerts feature to know their target typed the request for participating.

 

Screen time control

Monitr understands the parents’ worries about how much time a child spends on that particular app. Besides, WhatsApp, Viber or Telegram chatting became a great distraction machine. Devices stuffed with apps literally devour time and harm health because it is usually at the expense of eyesight, outdoor activities and sleep hours.

From now on, parents can manage the device and apps usage. They can limit access to certain apps, including social media messengers and Internet-based apps like YouTube. In a nutshell, get back your child to a school desk and to a family get-together.

 

What makes Monitr a stronghold on the monitoring market?

 

Monitr covert operations app included all angles of the monitoring. The device usage becomes visible to a caregiver, which provides tangible chances for an effective parenting. By using it, parents turn the device and the Internet into safe platforms for education and fun. The websites become healthy sources for the mental and intellectual development of your child.

Besides, this is one of the few covert operations application companies which provides 24/7 customer support in 8 languages. The staff offers a sort of mAssistance, accompanying from the installation process till the complete usage. The software is a sort of digital assistant helping parents with their daily routines, releasing time for what is more important for them.
